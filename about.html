Summer Scholarship Program


This summer, small groups of high school students will review a specific aspect of cancer research and develop a question that can be answered through the access of peer reviewed science journals and online databases. Students who are interested in participating can apply for a free summer hybrid program. Weekly Zoom and in-person group meetings will take place in the Santa Clarita area, culminating with a visit to the UCLA Liquid Biopsy Laboratory on Friday, July 21, 2023, where student groups will present their project in front of a panel of UCLA professors and research professionals. A tour of the laboratory along with a meet-and-greet will be provided by our sponsor, UCLA Liquid Biopsy Laboratory.
